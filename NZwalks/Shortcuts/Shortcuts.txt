Collaps & Expand:
	- Ctrl + M + O (Collapse)
	- Ctrl + M + P (Expand)

Search file:
	- Ctrl + ;

Multi Select:
	- Shift + Alt (press down arrow) (Same tab widht)
	- Ctrl + Alt (select positions as your wish)

Go to class:
	Ctrl + F12

Debugging:
	Fn + F10 (next step)
	Fn + F11 (goto function definition)

Surround with any blocker for selected content(Ex: try/catch):
	Ctrl + k + s
